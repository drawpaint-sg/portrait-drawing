Your Portrait Drawing Journey

Facial Proportions & Structure

Master the fundamental proportions and underlying structure of the human face.

  • Basic facial proportions & measurements
  • Skull structure & bone landmarks
  • Placement of features (eyes, nose, mouth)
  • Head construction from multiple angles
👁️

Feature Study & Anatomy

Detailed study of individual facial features and their anatomical structure.

  • Eye anatomy & construction
  • Nose structure & shadow patterns
  • Mouth & lips from different angles
  • Ear construction & detail work
💡

Light & Shadow Mastery

Understanding how light reveals form and creates dimension in portrait drawing.

  • Light source analysis & setup
  • Core shadows & cast shadows
  • Reflected light & halftones
  • Form modeling techniques
🎯

Likeness & Expression

Capture not just accurate proportions but the unique character and expression of your subject.

  • Observational drawing techniques
  • Capturing individual characteristics
  • Expression through feature placement
  • Personality in portrait work
✏️

Drawing Techniques & Media

Master various drawing materials and techniques for portrait work.

  • Graphite pencil techniques
  • Charcoal application & blending
  • Adding colour with chalk and sanguine
  • Paper selection & surface preparation
🎨

Finished Portrait Creation

Bring together all skills to create complete, professional-quality portrait drawings.

  • Composition & background treatment
  • Final refinement techniques
  • Presentation & portfolio development
  • Critique & artistic growth
